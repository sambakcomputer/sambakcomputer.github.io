Access Control

Access control systems are essential in ensuring the security of your business premises, homes etc. We understand the need to restrict access to important assets. Our installation and maintenance of access control systems allow you to determine who is allowed entry to certain areas of your premises with the aid of tokens. Talk to us about your access control needs.

Fire Alarm

Imagine a significant bundle of your assets, documents giving off crack sounds as they twist and transform to ash in a raging fire. There is need to protect your assets from destruction..planned or unplanned. A fire alarm will warn you, before you lose it all, before the need for investigation from your insurance company, before you are forced to start from scratch. Talk to us about detecting that raging fire.

Time and Attendance

Punctuality is the soul of business. - Thomas Chandler
Lateness to work can push productivity backwards significantly. Can you trust the security personnel or individual staff members to write down the correct clock in/out times? We will help you install a biometric machine capable of storing in its memory, non-falsified records of staff clock-in and clock-out times. Talk to us about your need for obtaining accurate punctuality records.

Burglar Alarm

Yes, fire outbreaks mostly occur out of the blue and they can be detected early, thanks to Fire alarms. But you know what else strikes out of the blue? Burglars! You practically don't know the exact time they'll come, so why not leave the job of detecting burglars to a burglar alarm? Talk to us about your need for automated burglar detection .

Door Video Phone

This device is mostly found in homes, though it can be used in other places. You might be having growing security concerns about your private space. We will help you install and maintain a door video phone that works without telecom charges to satisfy your security concerns, to identify the entity at the other side of the door. Talk to us about your need for a door video phone.

Intercom Phone

This device is quite common where workers have to interact over different areas in a building. Utilizing telecom services for such communication could be inefficient because of the need for constant communication in the same building. With LAN cables, we can help you install and maintain intercom phones that can send your audio message to anyone in the building for free. Talk to us about your need for an efficient and costless way to communicate.

Solar Inverter

A back-up power source i.e an inverter can come in handy anywhere, especially in cases where power supply is erratic. It can automatically step in once power goes off from the mains...without you even realizing. We will help you install and maintain an inverter that will direct power from various power sources such as solar panels or even the usual electric power lines, to batteries for storage and supply. Talk to us about maintaining stable power.
